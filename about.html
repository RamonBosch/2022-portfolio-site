About me

I have been employed as graphic designer for six years in an app development startup, an international hotel management company and a branding agency. I have been working on graphic design projects for ten years. I have developed and designed a web app in Javascript called Visualittle and a mobile game in Unreal Engine for Android and iOS called Ice Cream Disaster.

My full name is Ramon Bosch Pérez and I was born in Barcelona in 1990. After completing a bachelor's degree in arts I studied a four-year Grade in Graphic Design, a Postgraduate Degree in Graphic Design and another Postgraduate Degree in Branding.

I have been learning HTML, CSS, Javascript coding and Unreal Engine as well as 3D modeling with Blender by myself for the last couple of years.

I have lived abroad for more than two years in France, England and Finland so I can speak and write English easily and some French too.
