Castle Cluster

A card game about destroying your opponent's castles while keeping yours safe. Castle Cluster can be played one on one with a 50 card deck, and two more players can join for every extra deck, as turns happen simultaneously.

When the turn starts, every player has to play three cards facing downwards and choose another player to target. If the player's shields can't defend all attack points when the cards flip, the castles take the damage. There are also five special cards that may turn the tables when played at the right time.

Traduki

A vocabulary exchange game designed so it can be customized with the two languages the player chooses. It can be played by a single player and up to four different players. The players use the tokens to jump from one card to the next and try guessing the translation of the words each one of them contain.

If they guess right, they can keep guessing up to a maximum of three cards and get a bonus for every extra correct guess. Failing leaves the player on the same card and finishes the turn. The player with the most points at the end of the game wins.

Goal: The Match

I was commissioned to design this card game that simulates a soccer match. The player is actually the coach that plans a strategy for the soccer players to perform against another coach with his own set of players.

Each coach has a field board and they both share a scoreboard that signals the current result and minute of the match. As for the soccer players, each one has their own position on the field, and they perform better if they're in the right area.
