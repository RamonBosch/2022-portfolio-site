LinkedIn App Redesign

2021 | Mobile UI design and 3D modeling

Personal project. I redesigned some key features of LinkedIn's current app. This served as an exercise to help me better understand the process behind UX and UI design.

I had the chance to think about some elements that add up to and improve the current app, such as a comprehensive and smooth onboarding sequence, a simpler and cleaner information hierarchy and the addition of a dark mode.

Blender, Illustrator, Figma, After Effects, Photoshop
