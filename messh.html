Messh

2021 | 3D modeling and brand design

Personal project. Messh was a little experimental project I developed to explore how 3D modeling could be mixed with rand design.

Messh is a hackathon for the inexperienced in 3D modelling software. The goal of the event is to encourage those who want to start learning 3D to make mistakes and have fun while learning this new skill.

Blender, Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, Dimension
