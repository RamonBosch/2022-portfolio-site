Foliage

A fictitious premium tea brand. Foliage brings closer the smell of the best fresh leaves and timber by mixing them with all sorts of natural and organic ingredients.

I began by sketching ideas on the package design, and then developing the logo and icon based on the label's shape. Then, I edited the ten pictures that would be the background for each tea can and designed each one of the ten labels.

Using Adobe Dimension, I created 3D meshes for each can and rendered different scenes to showcase them. I also designed a website mockup using Illustrator and Photoshop.
