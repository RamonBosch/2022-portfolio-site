Corsicana

Corsicana is a fictitious brand of premium skincare products. Prepared by following an all-natural recipe, Corsicana's products bring the benefits of the healthy Mediterranean sun to your skin.

I began the project by sketching versions of the icon shape, and then, tracing and composing the rest of the logo in Illustrator. Once the logo was ready, I used the same process for the illustrations and coloured them using a simple four-colour palette.

I used the illustrations to design a pattern, and then proceeded to design the three packages and the rest of the mockups using both Illustrator and Photoshop.
