Tommy Mel's

2017 | Lettering, illustration and visual identity design

A rejected visual identity redesign. Tommy Mel's was a 70's American-vibe chain of restaurants born in Spain, and this is a re-imagining of their brand.

It's also my first lettering project ever. It was really fun to bring to life this little world of American cliches.

Illustrator, After Effects, Photoshop, InDesign
